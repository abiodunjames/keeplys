Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

DigitalKeep ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use DigitalKeep, we may collect:

  • Account information (email address, name) when you sign in with Apple or Google
  • Documents and images you scan or upload
  • Usage data and app analytics

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Process and store your documents
  • Enable cloud synchronization when you opt-in
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate important updates

Data Storage & Security

Your documents are stored locally on your device by default. When you enable cloud sync:

  • Documents are encrypted in transit
  • Data is stored in your personal iCloud or Google Drive account
  • We do not have access to your cloud-stored documents

Third-Party Services

DigitalKeep integrates with:

  • Apple Sign-In for authentication
  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • iCloud for optional cloud storage
  • Google Drive for optional cloud storage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your documents
  • Opt-out of cloud synchronization

Children's Privacy

DigitalKeep is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
