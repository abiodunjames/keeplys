Account Deletion Request

Last updated: December 2025

Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Keeplys account and all associated data, you can submit a request using the form below.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion, the following will occur:

  • Account Information — Your email, name, and authentication data will be permanently deleted from our systems
  • Cloud Sync Data — Any metadata associated with your cloud sync will be removed
  • Local Documents — Documents stored locally on your device will remain on your device unless you manually delete them
  • iCloud/Google Drive — Documents stored in your personal iCloud or Google Drive remain under your control and are not affected

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Before You Request Deletion

Please consider the following before submitting your request:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use cloud sync features in the future
  • Export any documents you wish to keep before requesting deletion

Submit Your Request

To request deletion of your Keeplys account, please fill out the form below. You will need to provide the email address associated with your account.
