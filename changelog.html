v1.1.0 Jan 3rd, 2026

Version 1.1.0

  • Uses iOS Vision and Android ML Kit for accurate, real-time document boundary detection
  • The scanner automatically identifies document edges as you point your camera
  • Fine-tune detected corners before saving for perfect crops
  • Automatically straightens tilted documents
  • Collapsed onboarding screens to one screen
v1.0.3 Dec 17, 2025

Version 1.0.3

  • Folder Selector in Scanner: See all your folders right in the scanner view.
  • Swipe to Switch: Swipe left or right on the camera view to quickly cycle through folders.
  • Visual Feedback: Each folder shows its custom color and icon, making it easy to identify at a glance.
  • ID Cards Default Folder: We've added ID Cards as a built-in default folder alongside Documents and Invoices.
  • Smart Frame Switching: The scanner now automatically adjusts its capture frame based on the selected folder.
  • No more manually switching between document and ID modes!
v1.0.2 Dec 13, 2025

Version 1.0.2

  • Cloud Sync: Automatically backup documents to Google Drive or iCloud Drive
  • Background Sync: Documents sync even when the app is closed
  • Document Upload: Import existing photos and PDFs from your library
  • Multi-language: Now available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Italian
  • Redesigned Settings: Cleaner interface for managing sync and storage
  • Bug fixes and performance improvements
